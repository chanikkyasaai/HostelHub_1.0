About MIT Hostels

Welcome to the official website of MIT Hostels. We provide comfortable and convenient accommodation for MIT students. Explore our hostels, facilities, and upcoming events. Contact us for any inquiries.

Welcome to MIT Hostels, the premier residential facility for students at Madras Institute of Technology. We aim to provide a comfortable and convenient living experience for our students. Our hostels offer a range of services, including:

  • Laundry services - Quick and efficient laundry service with user-friendly request system.
  • Store - Well-stocked store offering essentials and daily use items.
  • Maintenance - Easy reporting system for any maintenance issues in the hostel.
  • Outpass Request - Convenient request system for outpass permissions.
  • Docuserve - Seamless request process for availing academic documents.
  • Digital ID Card - Access your digital ID card from the portal.
  • Cards - Explore various cards and services offered by the hostel.
  • Guest House Booking - Book guest house accommodation for visitors.
  • Contact Us - Reach out to us for any inquiries or support.
Explore our website to learn more about our offerings and make the most of your hostel life!
